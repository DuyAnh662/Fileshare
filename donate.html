Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?
Yes, 100% free. You don't pay anything. You simply view content, and in return, you get more upload capacity. The ad revenue helps us keep FileShare running.
How long does each task take?
Each task takes approximately 30 seconds to complete. You can do multiple tasks whenever you have time.
When does my tier expire?
Supporter tier (5 completions) lasts for 1.5 months. Premium tier (50 completions) lasts for 1 full year. After expiration, you return to the free tier with 30 uploads/month.
Can I upgrade from Supporter to Premium?
Yes! Your completion count is saved permanently. If you continue completing tasks to reach 50, you'll be upgraded to Premium tier automatically.
Why should I support FileShare?
FileShare is a free service that costs money to run. By completing tasks, you help cover server costs and keep the service free for everyone. Plus, you get more uploads as a thank you!
