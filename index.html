Introduction to FileShare All Free

FileShare All Free is a non-profit project aimed at helping the community share and download files for free, ad-free, and without charging. We connect people through safe and convenient sharing of resources.

How it works

3 steps to share your file

1. Upload to Drive

Upload file to your personal Google Drive. Set the sharing permission to "Anyone with the link".

2. Share Link

Copy the link and paste it into the upload form. Fill in the name, description, and file type.

3. Wait for Approval

Admin will check and approve the file. After approval, the file will be displayed in the public list.
